Game Guide

How to Play

Pokemon Battle Royale is an auto-battler where 251 Pokemon from Gen 1 & 2 fight in a free-for-all arena until one remains. Choose your roster size, predict a winner, and watch the chaos unfold.

Pokemon move automatically, targeting the nearest opponent. They use auto-attacks to build energy, then unleash special moves when their energy bar is full. Eliminating opponents earns kills, which trigger evolution and item drops.

Storm Circle

The arena shrinks as Pokemon are eliminated. The storm circle's radius decreases proportionally to the number of remaining fighters.

Pokemon caught outside the circle take 1% maxHP per second as damage and are pushed toward the center. The minimum circle radius is 120px.

Tournament Mode

Tournament mode splits the roster into groups that battle in rounds. Top performers from each group advance to the next round.

2 RoundsGroups fight in round 1, survivors battle in the finals
3 RoundsThree elimination rounds with progressively fewer fighters

Between rounds, a bracket screen shows which Pokemon advanced and which were eliminated.
